Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a seller?
Simply click the "Register" button on our homepage, fill in your business details, and you'll be ready to start selling in minutes. Registration is free and takes less than 5 minutes.
What are the commission rates?
We charge a flat 10% commission on all sales. You keep 90% of your revenue. There are no hidden fees or additional charges.
How do I receive payments?
We use Stripe for secure payment processing. You'll need to connect your Stripe account during registration, and payments are automatically transferred to your account.
Is my data secure on Nexteria?
Absolutely! We use industry-standard security measures including password hashing, JWT authentication, and protection against common attacks like SQL injection and brute force attempts.
Can I sell any type of product?
We welcome most legitimate products. However, we prohibit illegal items, counterfeit goods, and products that violate our terms of service. Please review our seller guidelines for more details.
How do I track my sales?
Your seller dashboard provides real-time analytics including total sales, earnings, commission breakdown, and sales history. You can access this anytime after logging in.
